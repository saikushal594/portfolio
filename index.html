About Me

Hello! I'm Sai Kushal R, a Computer Science Engineering student with a passion for building clean, responsive, and user-focused web applications. With hands-on experience in both front-end and back-end technologies, I specialize in creating practical solutions that blend modern design with performance and scalability.

I've worked on various real-time projects including a face recognition attendance system and a plagiarism checker, where I applied core concepts of computer vision, machine learning, and string matching. These experiences have deepened my understanding of real-world problem solving and honed my full-stack development skills.

My core strengths lie in problem-solving, attention to detail, and team collaboration. I enjoy learning continuously, exploring new tech stacks, and working in dynamic environments that challenge me to grow. I also take pride in clean code practices and efficient debugging.

I actively participate in student organizations, events, and leadership roles — notably as the President of Spandana Students' Association and a key organizer in cultural festivals like Saagnika-2025. These roles have sharpened my leadership, communication, and organizational skills.

My goals include working with innovative teams to build meaningful tech solutions, contributing to open-source communities, and eventually mentoring aspiring developers. I value integrity, collaboration, creativity, and a commitment to continuous improvement.

Outside the tech world, I'm a passionate cook who enjoys experimenting with recipes and flavors. I also love watching cricket and actively take part in inter-hostel tournaments. For me, both coding and cooking are creative processes — structured, yet full of possibilities!

I'm currently based in Bengaluru and open to exciting tech opportunities, collaborations, and learning journeys.
